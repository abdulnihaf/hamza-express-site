Our Legacy

106 Years on H.K.P. Road, Shivajinagar

In 1918, when Shivajinagar was still finding its feet, Hamza started a small eatery on H.K.P. Road. Word spread - about the ghee rice that glistened just right, the brain fry that regulars still queue for, the biryani that wasn't trying to be Hyderabadi or Lucknowi but something distinctly Dakhni.

Four generations later, we're still here. The Russell Market vendors stop by for lunch. The Commercial Street shopkeepers send their staff. The late-night crowd knows we're the real thing when everything else closes.

Hamza Express brings this to you - not a new menu, not a cloud kitchen experiment. The same food, from the same kitchen, prepared the same way. The only difference? You don't have to find parking in Shivajinagar anymore.
