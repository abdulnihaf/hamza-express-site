Our Heritage

From the Legendary Kitchens of Hamza Hotel

In 1918, Mr. Hamza opened a small eatery in the heart of Shivajinagar, Bangalore. What began as a humble establishment has become one of the city's most cherished culinary institutions.

Hamza Express is the QSR extension of Hamza Hotel — bringing that same legendary taste to the modern world. Heritage taste, delivered with modern convenience.
