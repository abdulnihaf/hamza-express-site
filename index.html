Our Legacy

Four Generations of Culinary Excellence

Hamza Express is not just a new brand — it's a modern facelift of a century-old legacy. We bring the legendary taste that Bangalore has trusted for over 100 years, now delivered to your doorstep.

What began as a humble eatery in 1918 has evolved into a beloved institution. Today, Hamza Express carries forward the same authentic recipes, the same uncompromising quality, refined over four generations.
