## 1. What is this?

Hamza Express has a WhatsApp bot that handles most customer messages automatically — orders, bookings, menu questions, directions. But some conversations need a real person:

- A customer says "I want to talk to someone"
- A customer complains about food or service
- A customer wants to cancel a paid order
- A customer asks something the bot can't confidently answer

When that happens, the bot **pauses itself** for that customer and sends you a WhatsApp message. You reply to the customer through a simple web page on your phone, and once the conversation is resolved you hand control back to the bot.

---

## 2. Your role

You are the **first line** of human support. When a customer needs a person, you get the ping. You have 5 minutes to start replying before it escalates to Basheer; 10 minutes before it escalates to Nihaf.

If you're busy, that's fine — just don't worry about it. Someone else in the chain will pick it up.

If you're available, reply to the customer as soon as you can. The faster the first reply, the happier the customer.

## 5. How to reply

### Step 1 — Tap the link in the ping

The link opens in your phone's browser. It takes you straight to the customer's conversation.

### Step 2 — Enter your Ops PIN

First time you use it each day, you'll see a PIN screen. Type **7604** and tap Unlock. Your PIN is remembered for the rest of the browser session (until you close the tab or tap Exit).

### Step 3 — Read the conversation

You'll see the full message history with the customer — their questions, what the bot has replied, and any recent orders or bookings.

At the top you'll see a **red banner** if this is still an open handoff, or a **green banner** once you or someone else has claimed it.

### Step 4 — Type your reply

Tap the text box at the bottom, type your reply, and tap Send. The customer will see your reply as a normal message from the Hamza Express WhatsApp — **they don't see your personal number**. From their side it's the same thread they've always been chatting with.

**The moment you hit Send, the case is "claimed" by you.** Basheer and Nihaf will stop getting pinged for it. If you walk away mid-conversation and need someone else to take over, just tell Nihaf on the staff group and he can release or re-escalate.

### Step 5 — Keep chatting

As the customer replies, their messages show up in the inbox automatically. The page refreshes every 8 seconds while you have it open. You can just keep replying back and forth until it's resolved.

## 6. Closing a case

When the conversation is resolved — refund given, booking confirmed, complaint handled — tap the **Release** button at the top right of the thread.

You'll see a box asking for an optional note (e.g. "refund issued", "booked table for 8 PM"). Type it if it's useful for records, or leave it blank.

Tap **Release** to confirm. Three things happen:

1. The bot resumes handling the customer's future messages automatically
2. The case gets marked as resolved in the records
3. You're sent back to the inbox list

If you forget to release, the bot will automatically resume after 24 hours. But it's better to release explicitly — the customer's next message gets handled properly.

## 7. The escalation cascade

If you don't reply within **5 minutes**, the case escalates to **Basheer** (he gets the ping on his phone too). If he doesn't reply within another 5 minutes, it goes to **Nihaf**. After that, if still no one has replied, Faheem (you) gets re-pinged and the cycle restarts.

**First person to reply claims the case.** Everyone else's pings for that case stop.

So:

- If you're free, reply fast — before Basheer wakes up.
- If you're busy, ignore it — Basheer will pick it up.
- If you're busy and you see Basheer already replied, great — you can move on.
- If you see an old unclaimed case in the inbox, feel free to claim it even if it wasn't originally assigned to you.

## 8. What kinds of things get escalated

The bot escalates to you when:

**Explicit handoff**
- Customer says "talk to someone", "speak to manager", "call me", "real person", "are you a bot"
- Customer taps "💬 Talk to a person" in any menu

**Complaints**
- Customer says things like "bad food", "rude staff", "stale", "want refund", "cheated", "cockroach", "food poison"
- Anything negative gets you involved immediately — we never let a complaint sit in a bot

**Future (not live yet)**
- Customer wants to cancel a paid order (refund needed)
- Large party bookings (more than 12 people) need human confirmation

**What DOES NOT escalate**
- Regular menu questions → bot answers with menu link
- Timings / location → bot answers with outlet nudge
- "Do you deliver?" → bot redirects to Swiggy / Zomato
- Small bookings → bot handles fully
- Orders → bot handles fully

## 9. Tips

**Be warm, be brief.** Customers escalate because the bot felt cold or they have something urgent. Your first reply should acknowledge them by name (visible at the top of the thread).

**Don't start from scratch.** Read the last few messages before replying — they usually tell you what the customer wants without you having to ask.

**If the customer is angry, don't argue.** Apologize first, then ask what they'd like us to do. Most complaints are resolved by a simple "So sorry — let me fix this, what would you like?"

**If you need to offer a refund or comp**, you can — just tell the customer you're issuing it, then tell Nihaf what you've done so he can follow up on the Razorpay side.

**If the customer wants dine-in or pickup**, remember the goal: drive them to the outlet. Confirm the time, give them directions, and if possible hold a table.

**If you don't know the answer**, it's fine to say "Let me check and get back to you" — then ask Nihaf or whoever has the info, and reply in the inbox once you know.

## 11. Troubleshooting

**"The inbox page won't load"**
Check you have internet. Try opening https://hamzaexpress.in in another tab — if that works, hard-refresh the inbox tab (pull down on the screen). If it still fails, tell Nihaf.

**"My PIN isn't working"**
Ask Nihaf to check your PIN on the HR system. If he reset it recently, the old one is dead. Your current PIN is always what's shown on hnhotels.in/ops/hr for your employee record.

**"The customer isn't receiving my replies"**
Rare, but it happens if Meta's API has a hiccup. Wait 30 seconds and try again. If it still fails, tell Nihaf.

**"I accidentally hit Release and the case wasn't resolved"**
No problem. The customer's next message will create a new session as normal. If the case was mid-refund, just message them back manually through the inbox — the bot won't interrupt as long as they don't say anything the bot would pick up.

**"I see a case that's not mine — can I take it?"**
Yes. Any active or claimed case is visible to everyone in the Ops team. The first to reply claims it.

**"Someone else started replying before me — do I need to do anything?"**
No. Their claim stops the cascade. You'll see their replies in the thread with a different bubble color — feel free to close that case view and move on.
