Honest answers

Common questions

Can I bring guests?

Cover count = the size of your party. T1 (Nano) = 1 cover, you alone. T2 (5K-15K) = 2 covers, you + 1 guest. T3 = 3 covers. T4-T6 = 4 covers. T7 = 6 covers.

What if my engagement rate is low?

We look for active engagement, not just follower count. Auto-decline kicks in below 0.5% ER. T4+ tiers (30K+ followers) need ER ≥ 1.5% for auto-approval; otherwise we review manually. Inflated follower counts won't get past us.

Do I have to post a specific way?

No script. We don't review drafts. Just be honest. Tag @hamzaexpressblr, use the geotag pin, post within 7 days of your visit. That's it.

Can I get cash instead of meal?

T1-T4 are barter-only — that's the deal. T5+ get cash on top of the meal (₹500 / ₹3,000 / ₹8,000 by tier). For pure-cash collabs at T7, we'll talk.

How soon can I come?

If T1-T4 with passing engagement, instantly — pick a slot in the next 21 days, you're booked. T5+ usually 24h review window. Eid (May 27) slots fill fast.

What if I have YouTube / TikTok too?

Tell us in the application — cross-platform reach gets noted, helps us host you better. Tier is computed from IG followers; multi-platform is a bonus.

What happens after I post?

Nothing. We don't track you, don't message you weekly, don't bug you. We watch the post organically. If we love it, we'll reach out about future collabs at higher tiers.
