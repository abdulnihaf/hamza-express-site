Welcome.
You're at the Hamza family table.

Hamza Hotel has been on HKP Road in Shivajinagar since 1918. A hundred and eight years. Four generations of the same family. Through partition, license raj, through Bangalore turning from a sleepy cantonment into the city we know today — the kitchen has not gone dark for a single evening.

This year we facelifted the room. The recipes did not move an inch. We're inviting a small set of Bangalore food creators to come sit at the table the city has been sitting at for over a century, taste what four generations have been protecting, and tell that story in their own voice.

The next few steps will take about ninety seconds. Every application is read personally by us — there's no auto-approve. We'll respond within twenty-four hours via WhatsApp + email.
