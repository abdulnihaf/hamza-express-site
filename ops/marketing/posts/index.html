How This Works — Read this first

This dashboard helps you post to Instagram, Facebook, and Google for Hamza Express.

Your daily job:

  1. Open this page — the next post is highlighted at the top
  2. Follow the 4 steps: Generate Image → Upload → Caption → Publish
  3. Do it 3 times — once for Instagram, once for Facebook, once for Google
  4. The post tracker updates automatically when you publish

Tools you'll use:

  • AI Studio (aistudio.google.com) — generates images from a text prompt you copy from here
  • This dashboard — publishes to Instagram and Facebook automatically
  • Google Business (business.google.com) — for Google posts (manual upload for now)

Reference images to upload to AI Studio each time:

  • Image-1-Logo-Brown-BG.png — Brand logo
  • Image-2-Menu-Cover.png — Design reference
  • Image-3-Social-Post-Reference.png — Style reference
  • Image-4-Food-Photo.jpg — Swap with the right dish photo per post (some posts don't need it)

These files are in: hamza-express-site/social-media/reference/
