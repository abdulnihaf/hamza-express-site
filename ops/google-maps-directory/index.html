Radius Ring Logic
Radius is not a single setting. It changes by query quality and Bangalore traffic reality.
Distance control

0-3 km Inner Ring

Use for broader open-now and near-me hunger. These users are close enough that "fastest valid option" can become directions.

food open now
biryani near me
restaurant open now

3-7 km Intent Ring

Use when the query has food-category strength: kabab, non-veg, halal, late-night food, Shivajinagar.

kabab near me
non veg open now
halal restaurant

7-10 km Destination Ring

Use only for HKP/Shivajinagar/late-night Bangalore destination terms, especially weekend and post-midnight behavior.

late night food bangalore
shivajinagar biryani
hamza
Do not use a 10 km radius for generic restaurant searches. Use 10 km only when the query proves the person is willing to travel to a known food destination.

Tomorrow Use Flow
Use this sequence before enabling any Maps-facing campaign.
Checklist

Decision Flow

1. Pick bucketOpen-now, heavy dish, destination night, halal, brand.
2. Pick radius3 km for broad, 7 km for strong dish/night, 10 km only for destination/brand.
3. Pick timeMatch customer state, not owner availability.
4. Pick proofFood, board, route, parcel, or seating proof must match the bucket.
5. Enable pausedOnly after guardrails and cockpit visibility are clean.

Owner Questions

Can this person come?If traffic/radius makes the visit unrealistic, do not bid.
Did they reveal intent?Open-now, dish, HKP, halal, or Hamza memory must be visible in the query.
Will Maps convince them?Listing proof must answer the doubt before the route tap.
Can we read the result?If there is no query/hour/radius/POS read, the spend is too blind.
