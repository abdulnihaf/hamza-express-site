Maps / Local Ads

Local Ads can serve through Search and PMax when location assets are linked. For HE the real owner KPI is directions/call/menu until Store Visits becomes eligible.

directionscallsmenu viewsGBP asset
Store Visits means Google estimates people who physically visited after interacting with ads. It is not manually createable here yet; Google controls eligibility. Until then, HE should read directions, calls, menu views, corrected local spend, hour, network and eventual POS lift as the practical measurement stack.

Kept As-Is: PMax Store Goals Base Layer
PMax is still useful, but its role is different. It is broad local presence and store-goal discovery, supported by the GBP location asset and Customer Match list.
Base discovery

What PMax should do

Keep HE visible across Google inventory, learn from local action goals, and use Customer Match as signal. It can support all eaters but should not pretend to isolate them surgically.

What PMax should not do

Do not use it to test late-night keywords, query negatives, or exact buyer psychology. It can spend into Display/Content and YouTube, so it needs observability.

How to read it

Use the cockpit guardrails: channel split, corrected local spend, hour split, Customer Match health, asset group state, Store Visits eligibility and conversions.
